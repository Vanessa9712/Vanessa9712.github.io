About cheryl

嗨，我是欣怡。

如果你問我，什麼是我這輩子最驕傲的事，那也許不是得到最佳電視主持人的表現，也不是在上海得到最佳電台廣播獎，不是辦了個人脫口秀，也不是寫了什麼劇本……

我最驕傲的，是可以和我的觀眾，聽眾，讀者一起成長，一起學習。這世界沒人完美，但通過刻意練習，我們絕對可以一起變得更好。

Hi, I’m Cheryl.

I’ve been working in front and behind the scenes for 15 years. TV, radio, stand-ups, music, screenwriting, lyrics, books, you name it. 

The proudest thing throughout the journey, I would say, is not award-related, but to grow and learn with every single person that follows me, supports me and stands with me.

Life is about practicing purposefully, I believe. I’m glad that we can become better people together.
